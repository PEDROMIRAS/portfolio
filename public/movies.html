Movie Ranking Project

The Best Movies According to Pedro

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Transformers: The Last Knight

This is my favorite movie of all time. I love the action scenes and the special effects are amazing. The story is also very interesting and keeps me on the edge of my seat.

Pacific Rim

This movie is a close second to Transformers. I love the giant robots and the monsters. The action scenes are also very well done and the story is very engaging.

Grown Ups

This movie is a comedy that I love to watch when I want to relax and have a good laugh. The cast is amazing and the jokes are hilarious.
